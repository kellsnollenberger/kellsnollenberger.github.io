About

Kells Nollenberger is a Brooklyn‑based bassist celebrated for combining the rhythmic drive of jazz with the melodic sensitivity of chamber music. He has performed with the Stephane Wrembel Band, toured globally with the international production of West Side Story, and appears weekly in New York’s most intimate listening rooms. When he isn’t on stage, Kells mentors the next generation of low‑end lovers through private instruction and university clinics.
