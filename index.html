About

I grew up on the shores of Lake Superior in Duluth, MN, cut my teeth on Oscar Peterson records, and now split my time between laying down upright bass lines across New York City and running a small urban winery. Over the past decade I’ve toured with everyone from bluegrass icon Jeff Austin to the international cast of West Side Story, while keeping a weekly residency with my trio Polite Company at the Beekman Hotel.

When I’m not on stage you’ll find me designing batch cocktails, experimenting with bitter amari, or navigating the wild world of NYC HDFC co‑ops. Life tastes best when art, hospitality, and community all pour from the same bottle.

Current Projects

  • Polite Company – vintage swing & hot jazz brunches every Sat/Sun, Beekman Hotel
  • Stephane Wrembel Band – Gypsy jazz guitar wizardry (alumni 2014‑2016)
  • Broadway pits & session work – subbing on Moulin Rouge! and more
  • Bluegrass outings – upright slap & harmony vocals with rotating barn‑burners
